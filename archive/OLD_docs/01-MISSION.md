# CarScout — Mission & Purpose

## What It Is

CarScout is a personal due-diligence tool for Danish car buyers. It automatically scrapes listings from Bilbasen (DK) and Autoscout24 (DE), computes the true total cost of ownership (TCO) for every car across every scenario that applies to a Danish buyer, and presents the results in a searchable, comparable dashboard.

## Why It Exists

Buying a car in Denmark is unusually complex:

- **Registration tax** can exceed the car's purchase price — a 250,000 EUR German EV can cost 600,000+ DKK on the road in Denmark
- **EU imports look cheap** but carry VAT complexity, import costs, and registration tax risk
- **Flexleasing hides true cost** — the monthly payment bundles depreciation, interest, registration tax, and margin without transparency
- **Depreciation varies wildly** by model, fuel type, and generation
- **The right answer changes completely** depending on private vs. company ownership and how long you keep the car

Most people make this decision with a spreadsheet or gut feeling. CarScout replaces that with a system that computes the **true total cost of ownership** for every listing, across every scenario.

## Mission Statement

> Find the cheapest real-world cost of driving a specific car for 2, 3, or 5 years — not the sticker price.

## Core Differentiator

The only car search tool in Denmark that leads with TCO rather than sticker price. Sticker price in EUR is nearly meaningless for a Danish buyer — TCO is what you actually pay.

## Target User

A Danish professional buying or leasing a car, either privately or through a company, who wants to make a data-driven decision rather than relying on a dealer's framing.
